Date: April 03, 1996
Time: 1452
Location: Near Dubrovnik, Croatia
Operator: Military - U.S. Air Force
Flight #: 21
Route: Tizla - Dubrovnik
AC Type: Boeing B-737-T43
Registration: 73-1149
cn / ln: 20696/347
Aboard: 35   (passengers:29  crew:6)
Fatalities: 35   (passengers:29  crew:6)
Ground: 0
Summary: The aircraft strayed off course and hit St. John Hill at an altitude of 2,200 feet, 16km SE of Dubrovnik while attempting to land in poor weather conditions. U.S. Secretary of Commerce, Ron Brown, 55, killed. Crew error. Failure of the crew to comply with directives that required a review of all instrument approach procedures, not approved by the Defense Dept. Preflight planning errors, combined with errors made during the flight by the crew. Rumors persist that the accident was a result of sabotage.
