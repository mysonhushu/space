- ACCIDENT DETAILS
Date: March 28, 1998
Time: 0935
Location: Piura, Peru
Operator: Fuerza Aérea del Peru
Flight #: 388
Route: Tumbes - Piura
AC Type: Antonov AN-32
Registration: OB-1388
cn / ln: 1203
Aboard: 55   (passengers:50  crew:5)
Fatalities: 22   (passengers:22  crew:0)
Ground: 1
Summary: A Peruvian air force plane carrying villagers stranded by flooding crashed while attempting to land at Piura Airport. The pilot tried to glide the plane after losing an engine but crashed near a shantytown 2 kilometers from the airport. The plane began losing altitude after an engine failed and crashed into a drainage canal and broke in two. Engine failure.
