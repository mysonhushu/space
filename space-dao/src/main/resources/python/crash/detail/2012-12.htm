Summary: The plane disappeared after being taken on an illegal flight. A group of people were having a party at the airfield near the city of Serov, Russia. They managed to illegally steal an An-2 plane parked at the airfield. The plane went missing and was found on April 4, 2013, 8 km from Serov. Among those killed were the Chief of Serov city police, three police inspectors, the guard of the airport, a private company guard, a pensionist, a young unemployed man and a local mobile shop owner. The pilot was intoxicated at the time of the accident.
