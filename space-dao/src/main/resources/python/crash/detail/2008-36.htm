Date: August 24, 2008
Time: 2030

Operator: Itek Air

Route: Bishkek - Tehran, Iran
AC Type: Boeing B-737-219
Registration: EX-009
cn / ln: 22088/676
Aboard: 90   (passengers:83  crew:7)
Fatalities: 68   (passengers:67  crew:1)
Ground: 0
Summary: The plane crashed shortly after taking off from Manas International Airport. After 10 minutes, the crew reported a severe loss of cabin pressure and said they were returning when it crashed 6 miles from the airport. The Iran Aseman Airlines flight was being carried out by Itek Air. The forward left cabin door was not closed completely, and that the person who closed it was an engineer who was not listed among those (in the Cabin Attendant Manual) who were supposed to close the door. A report concludes that the aircraft originally lost cabin pressure due to this.
